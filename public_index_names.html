CCRG@RIT Catalog of Numerical Simulations


Presented here is a catalog of numerical simulations performed by the
CCRG@RIT numerical realtivity group using the moving puncture approach.
The l=2, 3, and 4 modes of Ψ4 and the strain extrapolated to infinite
observer location are included for download. Information given in the table below includes,
successively, a distinct identifier tag, a tag identifying the type of
configuration, a tag identifying a global resolution factor, simple
proper and coordinate separations, the mass ratio and spins of the
black holes, the starting frequency of the 2,2 mode in code units, merger time
and number of cycles in the 2,2 mode, and the final properties of the black
hole: mass, spin, recoil, and peak luminosity.

Release 1 of the catalog is now published: CQG 34, 22, 224001 (2017)
Release 2 of the catalog is now on the arxiv: arxiv:1901.02553
